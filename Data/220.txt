Muzaffarnagar
Aug 18, 2011
Dowry Harassment
Description:
			A local court here on Thursday convicted four persons and sentenced them to seven years of imprisonment in connection with a dowry case.

Additional District Sessions Judge M K Shukla also imposed a fine of Rs 2,000 each on victim Shammu's husband Kayyum, mother-in-law Wakila, sister-in-law Firdos and another person Shehzad, after holding them guilty of abetting suicide by harassing the victim for dowry, prosecution sources said.

Shammu committed suicide at Barota village in the district on October 6, 2003, they said.
