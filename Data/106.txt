Karnataka
Jul 27, 2011
Murder
Description:
			The police have recovered the decomposed body of V. Vanajakshi (27) in Punganur village of Andhra Pradesh and arrested her husband, Vinay Kumar, and friend Somashekhar in connection with her murder. 

Vanajakshi, a resident of Gottigere near Bannerghatta National Park, has been missing from home since Friday. 

Body dumped 
The Hulimavu police suspect Vinay of killing his wife and dumping her body under a culvert at Punganur.
