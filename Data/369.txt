BERHAMPUR
Oct 02, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A tribal class V student was allegedly raped by her teacher at the residential Bhaluguda Ashram School in Orissa's Malkangiri district. 

According to the police, Samantra, who is married with three children, allegedly lured the girl with the promise of marrying her. 

As per the victim's statement, made before the police on Friday, which was considered an FIR, the teacher had been exploiting her since July. The matter came to light when the cook and some other employees of the school brought it to the notice of the headmaster, who informed the District Welfare Officer and Samantra was immediately transferred.
