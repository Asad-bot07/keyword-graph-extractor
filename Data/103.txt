New Delhi
Jul 24, 2011
Murder
Description:
			A youth was on Tuesday arrested for allegedly strangulating his sister-in-law who objected to his sexual advances, police said. 

Ashu Sharma (24) allegedly killed Mamta (30) at her residence on July 24 after she refused to have physical relations with him, DCP (Outer District) BS Jaiswal said.
Acting on a tip-off, Ashu was nabbed from his sister's house at Poothkalan area.

During interrogation, Ashu confessed to his crime and said he wanted to have physical relations with the victim, but she refused his proposal and in a fit of rage he smothered her and fled from the house, police said.
