Maharashtra
Oct 03, 2011
Rape
Description:
			A resident of Wagle Estate in Thane, Raju Choudhary (29), was arrested after a 20-year-old woman from Bhayander lodged a complaint of rape against him.

Choudhary brought the woman to Thane on the pretext of showing her a flat that she could rent. "He took her to a room near C P Tank in Wagle Estate, spiked her drink and raped her," Choudhary later threatened her with dire consequences if she told anyone of what had happened.
