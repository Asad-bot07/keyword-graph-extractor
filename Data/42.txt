Mangalore South
Jul 17, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			Mangalore South Police arrested three persons here on Sunday for allegedly driving a motorcycle to a women's hostel and abusing the inmates.

The police said that Wilson Mendonca (32), Ajay (36), and Mario Rodrigues (38) rode a motorcycle to a women's hostel near Highland Hospital at about 1.15 a.m. on Sunday and broke a windowpane of the building. 

They allegedly abused the inmates and told them to come out. They allegedly abused a person whose name was given as Kiran in the neighbourhood.
