Kanpur
Jul 21, 2011
Murder, Rape
Description:
			A 17-year-old girl was allegedly strangulated to death by a man who attempted to rape her at Bahawalpur area in Ghatampur village in Kanpur, police said today.

The body of the Class-X student was found by locals on the village outskirts with a noose around her neck, they said.

The incident took place yesterday when the girl had gone to fields to relieve herself and did not return for long.

Police said the accused attempted to rape her failing which he strangulated her to death, adding a villager is among the suspects.
