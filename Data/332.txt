Allahabad
Sep 20, 2011
Dowry Harassment, Crime
Description:
			A 25-year-old woman was allegedly burnt to death by her husband and in-laws for dowry under Nawabganj police station on Tuesday. The victim was identified as Ram Dulari, a resident of Adampur.

Station officer Nawabganj said Ram Dulari was married Vijay Patel in July 2008 and the couple have a child. Her father Chedi Lal lodged a case under Dowry Act against Vijay and his parents alleging that they had been harassing Ram Dulari for dowry.

The neighbours spotted the burnt body of Ram Dulari and informed the police. The police later sent the body for postmortem.
