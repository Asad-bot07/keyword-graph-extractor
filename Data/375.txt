Wadarwasti
Oct 03, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			The police case was registered after the woman's husband allegedly set her on fire when she turned down demands for her to bring money from her parents to purchase a motorcycle. The incident took place on Saturday morning. The victim was identified as Manisha Salunkhe (25), who lives behind Sai Baba temple in Wadarwasti. 

The police said Salunkhe and her husband Sunil got married some four years ago and the couple had a two-year-old son. According to the victim, her husband, his mother Malan and her brothers-in-law Nitin (23) and Sachin (21) harassed her in order to make her get money from her parents' home.
