Kalyan
Sep 19, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			A police constable with the Thane Rural Police and four of his family members have been booked on the charges of harassment of his wife -- herself a lady constable -- and attempt to kill her. Hari Desai, the accused, is attached to Kasara police station while his wife, Shobha, is with the Kalyan police. The couple, married for eight years, lived in Kalyan. Police said that they quarrelled often, as Hari suspected the character of his wife. She alleged that on Friday, he tried to pour phenyl into her mouth, in an attempt to kill her. Bazar peth police at Kalyan have registered an offence against Hari and four other members of the family, for harassment for dowry, and other offences. No arrest has been made yet
