Banasawadi
Oct 02, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Murder, Rape
Description:
			The third Fast Track Court (FTC) has awarded life imprisonment to a 22-year-old man, Manjunath, for the rape and murder of a two-and-half-year-old girl. The crime was committed in Banasawadi village near Nelamangala in 2010.

Awarding the verdict on September 24, judge Narayana V Hegde also slapped a fine of Rs55,000 on Manjunath. KB Satyanarayana was the public prosecutor in the case. The incident took place on May 9, 2010. 

A man identified as Eerana came to Manjunath's house with his two-and-half-year-old girl at 8 pm on May 9. The girl fell asleep while watching TV. Manjunath then told Eerana that he would drop the toddler home. He then took her to an open field and raped and smothered her. Manjunath then dumped the body in a well.
