Kinathukadavu
Aug 19, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			Kinathukadavu police are on the look out for six persons who have reportedly harassed a 30-year-old woman sexually, near Verappagoundanur. 

In a complaint, the woman said that she had two children and her husband died an year ago. 

She said that six persons allegedly assaulted her sexually. 

She has been admitted to a private hospital. 

The police have registered a case. Probe is on.
