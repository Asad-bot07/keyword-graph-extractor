Sikandarpur
Oct 05, 2011
Abuse
Description:
			46-year-old housewife committed suicide on Tuesday night at her residence in Jajauli village in Sikandarpur (Ballia).

The victim was identified as Sunita Gupta, 46. According to reports, after dinner Sunita's husband and her two kids went to sleep in the veranda of the house while she stayed in her room. After midnight she locked the room from inside and hanged herself from the ceiling.

The incident came to light on Wednesday morning when she did not open the door of her room. The matter was reported to the police. Sunita's father, Shiv Shankar, has alleged that she committed suicide due to harassment by her husband and in-laws.
