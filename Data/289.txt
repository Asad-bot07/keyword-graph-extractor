Sep 10, 2011
Child Sexual Abuse, Rape
Description:
			A man was today arrested for allegedly raping a mentally challenged teenaged girl in Jammu and Kashmir's Ganderbal district, police said.

Police arrested 38-year-old Mohammad Hanif after locals accused him of raping the girl at Nagbal, 24 km from here.

According to the locals, the girl was in a paddy field when the man took her away to a nearby place and raped her.
